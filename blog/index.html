How to get water out of iPhone

Dropped your phone in the shower, and now your speakers sound muffled? There's multiple easy steps you can take to get rid of water from your phone and fix your speakers.

  1. Dry It With a Lint-Free Towel
  2. Shake phone to get excess water droplets off
  3. Go to Water Ejector
  4. Tap "Eject Water"
  5. Wait 30 seconds and your phone will be clear of any water

Why this works?

A pure sine wave sound is produced at 165hz which is perfect for shaking your speakers and removing excess water.
