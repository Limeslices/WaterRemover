Fix iPhone muffled speakers from water damage (2021)

Heres a 4 easy ways you can fix your iPhones muffled speakers.

Look it happens, you drop your phone in the sink, pool, or toilet. After you may notice your speakers sound slightly muffled, and you just can't get the sound to go away. Most modern iPhones are water-resistant, but that doesn't stop your speakers from being affected. You need to eject this water out of your phone or you could seriously damage your phone.

Now there's no official way to fix muffled speakers on your iPhone, this guide will show you the best way to quickly eject water from your device in just a few simple steps.

How is this possible?

This is all made possible through a simple website, remove-water.com that plays a special loud tone that forces all of the trapped water from out of your speakers. These droplets shoot out of your phone and will resolve all of your speaker problems. This is not just limited to water, but sand and dirt will also be pushed out of the way and help keep your speakers healthy.

4 steps to fix muffled iPhone speakers

Use the Remove-Water website
  1. Go to remove-water.com on your damaged iPhone
  2. Place your phone onto a flat surface. Gravity can help so try to place the iPhone on an incline.
  3. Tap the blue "Fix My Speakers" button in order to start the process.
  4. Wait 30 seconds, your muffled speakers will finally be clear!

Other Ideas

  • Place your phone in rice: Everyone knows this simple trick, but the thing is, it works. Keeping your phone in rice overnight can help draw out any excess water stuck in your device causing your speakers to be muffled.
  • User a fan or a hairdryer: Even Apply recommends using a fan or a blower to help progress water evaporation.
  • Apple support: If you think your iPhone is far too broken and you've done all you can. You can contact Apple Support. You can also take your device to the apple store for a professional to help you.
